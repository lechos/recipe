Tamari Stir-Fry

A quick and simple stir-fry recipe that goes great with brown rice or rice noodles!

prep time: 10 mins | cook time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 cups broccoli
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 3 cups crimini mushrooms
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 package extra-firm tofu
  • 3/4 cup of cashews
  • 1/2 teriyaki sauce
  • 1/2 cup tamari sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse broccoli, peppers, and mushrooms. Chop broccoli and slice peppers and mushrooms.
  2. Put vegetables into large mixing bowl. Pour in teriyaki, tamari, and soy sauces sprinkle in sugar and spices and stir well.
  3. Chop onion and saute with olive oil on medium heat until onions begin to turn clear. Slice tofu into cubes and brown with onion mixture.
  4. Add vegetable mixture to tofu and onions. Reduce heat and let simmer with lid on for 10 minutes, or until vegetables begin to soften. Break cashews into smaller pieces if desired, and stir into stir-fry. Simmer on low heat with lid for another 10-20 minutes.
  5. When vegetables are soft, remove from heat and let stand without lid for 5 minutes. Serve by itself, or with brown rice or noodles if desired!
